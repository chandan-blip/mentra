Questions

Everything you're wondering

Who is Mentra for?
Computer Science graduates and final-year students — B.Tech, BCA, B.Sc CS, MCA, and Diploma — who want their first Software Developer job. Whether you have no interview calls yet or just don't know how to prepare, Mentra gives you a clear plan and real mentorship.
Is Mentra a course?
No. You've already finished your degree — Mentra isn't another course to sit through. It's mentorship that shows you exactly how to crack the interview: the questions HR asks, what technical rounds actually test, how to present your projects, and how to get shortlisted.
How does the interview-prep plan work?
After a short onboarding, Mentra builds a step-by-step plan to crack your target role — getting your resume shortlisted, preparing for common HR questions, handling the tasks technical rounds give, and rehearsing with mock interviews. As you complete each step, it guides you to the next, so you're never guessing.
Will this actually help me get placed?
Mentra is built around interview outcomes, not just lessons. You practice real interview questions daily, sit mock technical and HR rounds, get your resume reviewed, and track every application — so you walk into interviews prepared, not hoping.
Is it free to start?
Yes — your interview-prep plan, daily practice, and progress tracking are free to start. You can unlock live mentorship, mock interviews, and resume review as you grow.
