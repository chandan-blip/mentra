Questions

Good things to know

Who is Mentra for?
Anyone building toward a role — first job, career switch, leveling up, or interview prep. You tell Mentra your goal in onboarding and it shapes everything around it.
Do I need experience to start?
No. The roadmap is built around your current level — whether you're starting from zero or filling specific gaps, the plan meets you where you are.
How does the AI roadmap actually work?
After onboarding, Mentra generates a week-by-week plan tailored to your goal and skills — each topic with its own subtopics and a completion test. As you finish milestones, it re-plots the path around what you've learned.
Is the mentor a real person or AI?
Your always-on chat coach is AI — it remembers your journey and answers anytime. Live sessions and bookings connect you with real human mentors when you want that.
Is it free to start?
Yes — your roadmap, AI mentor, and progress tracking are free to get started. You can unlock live sessions, deeper projects, and more as you grow.
